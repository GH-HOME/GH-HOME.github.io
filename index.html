About Me

I received my Ph.D. (Apr. 2019 - Mar. 2022) from Osaka University under the supervision of Prof. Yasuyuki Matsushita. Previously I obtained my Master and Bachelor degree in 2018 and 2015, both from University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC), under the supervision of Prof. Bing Zeng and Prof. Shuaicheng Liu. My research interest includes computational photograpy and computer vision.
